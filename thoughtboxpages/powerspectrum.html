A Noob's Intro to Power Spectrum'

The Power spectrum is a modern day cosmologist's magnum opus to parameter estimation problem. This is due to two primary reasons:

  • Gaussianity implies that all the odd n-point correlation functions vanish. Since all the higher order even n-point correlation functions can be written as a product of the 2-point correlation function, it is all that one needs to represent the data
