About Me

I am an Astrophysicist in the South Pole Telescope collaboration. I did my PhD in Dr. Christian Reichardt's Observational Cosmology group at the University of Melbourne. I work on topics related to the Cosmic Microwave Background . Though, I am interested in theoretical physics in general.


Previously, I was at the Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmedabad, where I worked with Prof. Subhendra Mohanty on cosmological models of self-interacting dark matter and Soft Graviton theorems.


Besides Physics, I am also interested in machine learning, data science, statstistics, finance, and related things. I occasionally write blogs on these topics. Please have a look below.
