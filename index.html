About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Dr. Christian Reichardt's Observational Cosmology group at the University of Melbourne. I work with the South Pole Telescope (SPT) Collaboration on the Cosmic Microwave Background and related topics.


Before joining this group, I was at the Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmedabad, where I worked with Prof. Subhendra Mohanty on cosmological models of self-interacting dark matter.


Besides Physics, I am also interested in machine learning, data science, statstistics, finance, and related things. I occasionally write blogs on these topics. Please have a look below.
