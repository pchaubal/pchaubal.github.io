About Me

I was an Astrophysicist in the South Pole Telescope collaboration. As fate would have it, I had to move out of academia, despite not wanting to do it. I now work in a Photonics Design startup called WavePhotonics as a Scientific Simulation Developer with amazing colleagues and fun work. I did my PhD in Dr. Christian Reichardt's Observational Cosmology group at the University of Melbourne. I work on topics related to the Cosmic Microwave Background. I am generally interested in all aspects of theoretical physics.


Previously, I was at the Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmedabad, where I worked with Prof. Subhendra Mohanty on cosmological models of self-interacting dark matter and soft graviton theorems.


I did my Masters in Physics from the University of Trento in the SISSA/Trento Joint Masters Program where I did my thesis work with Dr. Marco Raveri and Prof. Carlo Baccigaluppi on theories of modified gravity.


Besides Physics, I am also interested in machine learning, data science, statstistics, finance, and related things. I occasionally write blogs on these topics. Please have a look below.
