Hi,

I am a PhD candidate in Dr. Christian Reichardt's Cosmology group at the University of Melbourne. I am working with the South Pole Telescope (SPT) Collaboration on the Cosmic Microwave Background and related topics.

Prior to joining this group, I was at the Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmedabad where I worked with Prof. Subendra Mohanty.

I did my Masters in Theoretical Physics from the University of Trento under the SISSA-Trento Joint Masters program.
